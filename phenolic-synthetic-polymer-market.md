<p><ul><li>Thermoplastic Phenolic Synthetic Polymer</li><li>Thermosetting Phenolic Synthetic Polymer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The phenolic synthetic polymer market is divided into two main types: thermoplastic and thermosetting phenolic polymers. Thermoplastic phenolic synthetic polymers can be reprocessed and reshaped when heated, offering flexibility and easier handling in applications. In contrast, thermosetting phenolic synthetic polymers undergo a chemical change when cured, resulting in a rigid structure that is durable and heat-resistant, making them ideal for applications requiring stability and strength. Both types play vital roles in various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and electronics.</p></p>
