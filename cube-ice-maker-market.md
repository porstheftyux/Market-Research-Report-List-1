<p><ul><li>Water-Cooled Ice Machine</li><li>Air-Cooled Ice Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cube Ice Maker market primarily consists of two main types: water-cooled and air-cooled ice machines. Water-cooled machines use a continuous flow of water to dissipate heat, making them efficient in high-temperature environments. They are often used in larger establishments due to their cooling efficiency. Conversely, air-cooled machines rely on air circulation for cooling and are typically more energy-efficient and easier to install, making them popular in smaller venues and areas with limited water supply. Both types cater to diverse commercial ice-making needs.</p></p>
