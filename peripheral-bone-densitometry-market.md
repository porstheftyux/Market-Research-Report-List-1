<p><strong>The Peripheral Bone Densitometry Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Energy X-Ray Absorptiometry (SEXA)</li><li>Dual Energy X-Ray Absorptiometry (pDEXA)</li><li>Radiographic Absorptiometry (RA)</li><li>Quantitative Ultrasound (QUS)</li><li>Peripheral Quantitative Computed Tomography (pQCT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Peripheral Bone Densitometry market includes several technologies for assessing bone density. Single Energy X-Ray Absorptiometry (SEXA) uses a single energy source for measurements, while Dual Energy X-Ray Absorptiometry (pDEXA) employs two energies for enhanced accuracy. Radiographic Absorptiometry (RA) measures bone density through standard X-ray images. Quantitative Ultrasound (QUS) utilizes sound waves to evaluate bone properties, and Peripheral Quantitative Computed Tomography (pQCT) provides 3D imaging for detailed analysis of bone structure and density.</p></p>
