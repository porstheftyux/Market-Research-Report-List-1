<p><p>The Digital Pigment Ink market is defined by two main types: Micron Ink and Nanoscale Ink. Micron Ink contains larger pigment particles, offering robust color saturation and excellent lightfastness, ideal for various applications in textiles and graphics. In contrast, Nanoscale Ink features much smaller pigments, which provide finer detail, enhanced color vibrancy, and superior adherence to substrates. This differentiation allows industries to select the appropriate ink type based on specific performance requirements and desired print quality.</p></p>
