<p><p>The Cardiovascular Information Systems (CVIS) market comprises three main types: On-Site CVIS, Web-Based CVIS, and Cloud-Based CVIS. On-Site CVIS involves having software installed and maintained locally within healthcare facilities, offering control over data but requiring more IT resources. Web-Based CVIS operates through a web interface, enabling remote access and easier updates, making it user-friendly. Cloud-Based CVIS leverages cloud technology for data storage and management, offering scalability, reduced costs, and accessibility from diverse locations, enhancing collaboration in cardiovascular care.</p></p>
