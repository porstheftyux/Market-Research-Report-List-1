<p><p>The mango pulper machine market consists of two main types: batch pulper machines and continuous pulper machines. Batch pulper machines process mangoes in discrete batches, ideal for small-scale operations and varying production needs. In contrast, continuous pulper machines provide a steady, uninterrupted flow of mango processing, making them suitable for large-scale industrial production. Both types cater to different production capacities, operational efficiencies, and specific requirements in the food processing industry, ensuring diverse applications for mango pulp production.</p></p>
