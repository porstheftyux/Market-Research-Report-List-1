<p><strong>The Dosimetry Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>OSL Technology</li><li>TLD Technology</li><li>CR-39 Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dosimetry services market encompasses various technologies for measuring radiation exposure. OSL (Optically Stimulated Luminescence) technology uses aluminum oxide crystals that emit light when stimulated, providing precise dose measurements. TLD (Thermoluminescent Dosimetry) technology utilizes materials that release stored energy as light upon heating, allowing for dose estimation. CR-39 (Columbia Resin-39) technology involves a plastic polymer that tracks alpha particle exposure through etching patterns. Each technology offers distinct advantages for monitoring radiation levels in healthcare, industry, and research settings.</p></p>
