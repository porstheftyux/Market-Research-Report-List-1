<p><p>The TPE (Thermoplastic Elastomer) film market is segmented into ventilate and non-ventilate types based on breathability characteristics. Ventilate TPE films allow air and moisture to pass through, making them ideal for applications like medical devices and breathable textiles. In contrast, non-ventilate TPE films provide a shield against moisture and contaminants, making them suitable for packaging, protective gear, and other industries requiring barrier properties. Each type serves distinct needs, enhancing performance in specific applications.</p></p>
