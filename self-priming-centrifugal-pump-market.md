<p><p>The self-priming centrifugal pump market consists of two main types: multistage self-priming pumps and single-stage self-priming pumps. Multistage self-priming pumps are designed to handle high-pressure applications by utilizing multiple impellers, making them suitable for demanding tasks like water supply and industrial processes. In contrast, single-stage self-priming pumps operate with a single impeller, ideal for lower-pressure applications, such as household water systems and irrigation. Each type caters to specific operational requirements, influencing their market demand and applications.</p></p>
