<p><p>5G network planning and design encompass two main market types: interior and outdoor planning. Interior planning focuses on optimizing 5G coverage and capacity within buildings, ensuring efficient signal distribution through strategic placement of small cells and network equipment. In contrast, outdoor planning addresses broader coverage requirements, considering factors like urban density, terrain, and existing infrastructure. Both approaches aim to enhance user experience, reduce latency, and meet the growing demand for faster mobile connectivity, each tailored to distinct environmental challenges.</p></p>
