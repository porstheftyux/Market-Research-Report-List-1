<p><p>IV dressings are essential for securing intravenous lines and preventing infections. The market can be divided into two main types: transparent and translucent. Transparent dressings provide clear visibility of the insertion site, allowing for easy monitoring without removing the dressing. In contrast, translucent dressings offer partial opacity, providing a balance between visibility and reduced glare. Both types are designed to be breathable, waterproof, and adhesive, ensuring that IV access is maintained while protecting the site from contaminants.</p></p>
