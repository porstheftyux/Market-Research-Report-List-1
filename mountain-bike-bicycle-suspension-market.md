<p><ul><li>Hardtail Suspension</li><li>Full Suspension</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mountain bike suspension market primarily features two types: hardtail and full suspension. Hardtail bikes have a rigid frame at the rear and only a front suspension, offering lightweight efficiency and ideal for cross-country riding. In contrast, full-suspension bikes incorporate both front and rear shock absorbers, providing enhanced comfort and handling on rough terrains, making them suitable for downhill and trail riding. Each type caters to different rider preferences and terrains, influencing performance and overall riding experience.</p></p>
