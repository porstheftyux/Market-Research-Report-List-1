<p><p>The home air quality sensor market is primarily divided into two types: signal gas monitoring sensors and multi-gas monitoring sensors. Signal gas monitoring sensors focus on detecting specific pollutants, such as carbon monoxide or volatile organic compounds, providing targeted information about single gas levels. In contrast, multi-gas monitoring sensors assess various pollutants simultaneously, offering a comprehensive overview of indoor air quality. Both types are crucial for enhancing health and safety by enabling homeowners to identify and mitigate air quality issues.</p></p>
