<p><p>The medical sterile packaging technology market comprises two main sterilization types: chemical and physical. Chemical sterilization involves the use of chemical agents, such as ethylene oxide or hydrogen peroxide, to eliminate microorganisms from medical devices and packaging. In contrast, physical sterilization utilizes methods like steam, radiation, or dry heat to achieve sterility. Both approaches are essential for ensuring the safety and efficacy of medical products, catering to various applications in hospitals and healthcare settings, while maintaining product integrity during storage and transportation.</p></p>
