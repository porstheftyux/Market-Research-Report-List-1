<p><p>Concentrated Solar Power (CSP) technology harnesses solar energy through various methods. Parabolic trough collectors use curved mirrors to focus sunlight onto a receiver, heating a fluid that generates steam for electricity. Solar towers employ a central tower surrounded by mirrors to concentrate sunlight, with the heat used to produce steam. Other CSP technologies include dish-Stirling systems and linear Fresnel reflectors, each offering unique advantages in efficiency and scalability for energy generation in diverse environmental conditions.</p></p>
