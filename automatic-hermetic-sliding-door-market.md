<p><p>The automatic hermetic sliding door market is categorized into two main types: single leaf and double leaves. Single leaf doors consist of one pane that slides open, ideal for narrow spaces and low-traffic areas, providing efficient sealing against contaminants. Double leaf doors feature two sliding panels, offering wider openings suitable for high-traffic environments, such as hospitals and laboratories, where accessibility and sterilization are critical. Both types prioritize energy efficiency, infection control, and safety in sensitive applications.</p></p>
