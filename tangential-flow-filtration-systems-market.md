<p><p>Tangential Flow Filtration (TFF) systems are advanced filtration techniques widely used in bioprocessing and pharmaceutical applications to separate and purify biological molecules, such as proteins, nucleic acids, and cells. In TFF, the feed stream flows tangentially across the surface of a filter membrane, allowing for efficient separation without clogging, which enhances the filtration process. The systems are characterized by their ability to concentrate and retentate valuable products while efficiently removing impurities.</p><p>The Tangential Flow Filtration Systems Market is anticipated to grow at a CAGR of 9.5% during the forecast period, driven by the increasing demand for biopharmaceuticals, advancements in membrane technology, and a growing focus on process optimization. Additionally, the shift towards continuous processing in manufacturing is fostering the use of TFF systems for improved efficiency and cost-effectiveness.
