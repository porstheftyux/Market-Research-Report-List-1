<p><ul><li>Packed Towers</li><li>Tray Towers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Degasifiers are essential for removing dissolved gases from liquids, primarily in water treatment processes. The market includes two main types: packed towers and tray towers. Packed towers utilize structured packing to maximize the contact surface area between the liquid and gas, enhancing mass transfer and efficiency. Tray towers, on the other hand, employ a series of trays or plates to facilitate the gas-liquid interaction, allowing for efficient gas removal. Both types serve various industrial applications, including municipal and industrial water treatment.</p></p>
