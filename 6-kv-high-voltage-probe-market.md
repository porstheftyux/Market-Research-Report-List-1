<p><p>The 6 kV high voltage probe market consists of two primary types: single-end and double-end probes. Single-end probes are designed for connecting to one terminal of a high voltage system, offering simplicity and ease of use for measurements in less complex setups. Conversely, double-end probes connect to both terminals, providing improved accuracy and reduced common-mode noise in differential measurements. These distinctions cater to varying requirements in high voltage testing, diagnostics, and research across different applications and industries.</p></p>
