<p><p>Single-cell genome sequencing encompasses various market types, each leveraging distinct technologies. Next-Generation Sequencing (NGS) allows for high-throughput analysis of individual cell genomes, providing comprehensive insights. Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) amplifies specific DNA sequences from single cells for targeted studies. Quantitative PCR (qPCR) quantifies DNA or RNA levels, enhancing expression analysis at the single-cell level. Microarrays enable simultaneous detection of multiple genes, while Multiple Displacement Amplification (MDA) offers robust amplification of genomic DNA from single cells, supporting diverse genomic investigations.</p></p>
