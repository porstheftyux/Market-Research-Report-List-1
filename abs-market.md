<p><strong>The ABS Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Emulsion Graft Copolymerization</li><li>Bulk Copolymerization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ABS market comprises various polymerization methods, including emulsion graft copolymerization and bulk copolymerization. Emulsion graft copolymerization involves the polymerization of acrylonitrile and butadiene in an emulsion, resulting in a rubbery product with improved impact resistance and thermal stability. On the other hand, bulk copolymerization occurs by combining monomers without solvent, producing a denser and more homogeneous material. Both methods cater to diverse industries, including automotive, electronics, and consumer goods, enhancing the mechanical and aesthetic properties of plastics.</p></p>
