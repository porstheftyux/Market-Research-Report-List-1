<p><p>The Size Exclusion Chromatography (SEC) column market encompasses two primary types: Oil Soluble SEC, commonly referred to as Gel Permeation Chromatography (GPC), and Water Soluble SEC, known as Gel Filtration Chromatography (GFC). GPC is designed for analyzing non-polar, oil-soluble polymers, providing insights into molecular weight distribution, while GFC focuses on water-soluble compounds, suitable for biomolecules and polysaccharides. Both techniques utilize porous media to separate molecules based on size, ensuring effective purification and characterization in various applications.</p></p>
