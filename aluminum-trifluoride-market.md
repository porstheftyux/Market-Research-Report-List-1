<p><p>The aluminum trifluoride (AlF3) market is primarily segmented into two types: dry AlF3 and wet AlF3. Dry AlF3 is produced through anhydrous processes and is often used in aluminum smelting to enhance electrolyte performance, improving production efficiency. In contrast, wet AlF3 is manufactured using aqueous methods and is typically utilized in the chemical industry for various applications, including catalysts and ceramics. Each type caters to distinct industrial needs, driving its respective market demand.</p></p>
