The float chamber maintains the proper fuel level for consistent delivery. The orifice regulates the fuel-air mixture flow. The nozzle atomizes fuel for efficient combustion, while the throttle controls the engine's air intake and power output. Other parts may include gaskets and screws, contributing to overall functionality. Each component plays a critical role in optimizing engine performance and fuel efficiency.</p></p>
