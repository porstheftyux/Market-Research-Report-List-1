<p><p>RS232, RS485, and RS422 are standard communication protocols used for serial communication in various electronic devices. RS232 is widely recognized for short-distance communication, typically up to 50 feet, and is commonly used in PCs and printers. RS485 is more robust, supporting longer distances, up to 4,000 feet, and allows for multiple devices on a single network, making it ideal for industrial applications. RS422 offers similar advantages to RS485 but is primarily designed for point-to-point communication. Isolated transceivers enhance these protocols by providing electrical isolation, protecting devices from voltage spikes and ensuring stable communication in noisy environments.</p><p>The RS232, RS485, RS422 Isolated Transceiver Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.4% during the forecast period.
