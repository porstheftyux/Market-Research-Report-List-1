<p><p>Automated Fingerprint Identification Systems (AFIS) are biometric systems used to match fingerprints against a database for identification or verification purposes. The Single Modal AFIS relies solely on fingerprint data for recognition, offering simplicity and speed but may be limited by quality issues. Conversely, Multi Modal AFIS integrates various biometric data types, such as facial recognition and iris scans, enhancing accuracy and security. This adaptability allows for improved performance in diverse applications, making it suitable for complex identification needs.</p></p>
