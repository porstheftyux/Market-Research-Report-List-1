<p><p>Single-ply roofing membranes are widely used due to their durability and ease of installation. PVC (polyvinyl chloride) membranes are known for their resistance to chemicals and fire, making them suitable for a variety of applications. EPDM (ethylene propylene diene monomer) is favored for its excellent weather resistance and longevity, particularly in diverse climates. TPO (thermoplastic polyolefin) membranes combine the benefits of both PVC and EPDM, offering energy efficiency and UV resistance, making them increasingly popular in the roofing market.</p></p>
