<p><ul><li>All process OEM</li><li>Half process OEM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the cosmetics OEM market, there are two main types: All Process OEM and Half Process OEM. </p><p>All Process OEM involves complete product development, from formulation to packaging, allowing brands to offer unique products with minimal involvement in the manufacturing process. </p><p>Half Process OEM provides brands with partially developed products, where they take part in aspects like formulation or packaging design, thus allowing more customization while still leveraging the manufacturer's expertise. Both models enable brands to effectively manage production and innovate their offerings.</p></p>
