<p><p>The LDPE packaging market encompasses various types, notably shrink films and stretch films. Shrink films are designed to conform tightly around products when heat is applied, providing protection and enhancing shelf appeal. They are commonly used for multi-packs and unitization. In contrast, stretch films are stretchable and wrap around items, securing them for transport and storage. They offer superior load stability and resistance to puncturing, making them essential for palletizing and protecting goods in various industries.</p></p>
