<p><p>The extracorporeal membrane oxygenation (ECMO) system market is categorized into three main types: Veno-Arterial (VA), Veno-Venous (VV), and Arterio-Venous (AV). VA ECMO supports patients with heart and lung failure by providing oxygenated blood to the body while removing carbon dioxide. VV ECMO is primarily used for patients with severe respiratory issues, oxygenating blood without facilitating adequate systemic circulation. AV ECMO combines arterial and venous access for certain therapeutic applications, often in dialysis or medical procedures.</p></p>
