<p><p>In the New Energy Vehicles (NEVs) market, Brake By Wire (BBW) systems are classified into two primary types: Two-Box and One-Box systems. A Two-Box system separates the electronic control unit (ECU) and the braking actuator, allowing for greater design flexibility and redundancy for safety. In contrast, a One-Box system integrates both the ECU and actuator into a single unit, offering a compact design and potentially lower manufacturing costs. Each type serves specific performance and safety requirements in NEVs.</p></p>
