<p><p>The automotive smart headlights market includes types such as Adaptive Front-lighting Headlights and Adaptive Driving Beam Headlights. Adaptive Front-lighting Headlights adjust the beam direction based on vehicle speed and steering, enhancing visibility in curves and improving safety. Conversely, Adaptive Driving Beam Headlights utilize sensors to automatically control the intensity and direction of light, ensuring optimal illumination without dazzling oncoming drivers.
