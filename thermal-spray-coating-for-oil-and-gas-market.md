<p><p>Thermal spray coating in the oil and gas market encompasses various techniques used to enhance surface properties and extend equipment life. Cold spray employs supersonic gas to deposit particles at low temperatures, minimizing thermal stress. Flame spray utilizes a combustible gas for high-temperature coating application, ideal for wear resistance. Plasma spray generates a high-temperature plasma arc to melt and project materials onto surfaces, providing robust coatings. Other methods may include HVOF and HVAF, each tailored for specific industrial applications.</p></p>
