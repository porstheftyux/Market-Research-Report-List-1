<p><p>The non-metallic temperature sensor market comprises various types, primarily thermistor and thermocouple sensors. Thermistors are highly sensitive resistors that change resistance with temperature fluctuations, offering precise measurements in a range of applications, especially in medical devices and HVAC systems. Thermocouples, on the other hand, consist of two different metal wires joined at one end, generating a voltage based on temperature differences, suitable for high-temperature environments in industrial processes. Both types provide effective solutions for temperature monitoring across various sectors.</p></p>
