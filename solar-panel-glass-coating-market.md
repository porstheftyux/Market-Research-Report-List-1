<p><p>The solar panel glass coating market consists of two main types: hydrophilic and hydrophobic coatings. Hydrophilic coatings attract water, allowing dirt and grime to wash away easily during rainfall, thereby enhancing efficiency. In contrast, hydrophobic coatings repel water, causing it to bead and roll off the surface, which reduces dirt accumulation but may require manual cleaning. Both types play a crucial role in maximizing solar panel performance, ensuring better light transmission and longer operational life in various environmental conditions.</p></p>
