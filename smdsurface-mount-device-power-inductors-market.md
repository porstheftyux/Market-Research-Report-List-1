<p><p>The SMD power inductors market comprises three main types: wire-wound, multilayer, and thin-film. Wire-wound inductors utilize insulated wire coiled around a core, offering high inductance and better performance. Multilayer inductors consist of multiple stacked layers, allowing for compact designs with low resistance and high inductance. Thin-film inductors are produced using advanced deposition techniques, providing excellent performance in tight spaces with minimal footprint. Each type caters to different application needs in compact electronic devices, optimizing efficiency and space utilization.</p></p>
