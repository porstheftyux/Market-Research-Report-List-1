<p><p>The medical device sterilization market consists of various methods to ensure the safety and efficacy of medical instruments. Steam sterilization utilizes high-pressure steam to eliminate pathogens, making it highly effective for heat-resistant materials. Ethylene Oxide (EO) is a gas-based method suitable for heat-sensitive devices, allowing for thorough sterilization at low temperatures. Hydrogen peroxide vapor and plasma leverage reactive vapor to sterilize surfaces efficiently, making them ideal for complex instruments. Each method addresses unique sterilization needs in healthcare settings.</p></p>
