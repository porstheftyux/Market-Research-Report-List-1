<p><p>Well testing services comprise various market types essential for evaluating oil and gas wells. Down-hole testing involves conducting assessments directly within the wellbore to determine performance and fluid characteristics. Real-time well testing provides immediate data analysis during drilling operations. Reservoir sampling and analysis focus on gathering reservoir fluids to study reservoir behavior. Surface well testing evaluates surface equipment and production efficiency.
