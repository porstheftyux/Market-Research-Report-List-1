<p><p>The ostomy bags market consists primarily of two types: one-piece and two-piece systems. One-piece bags integrate the adhesive skin barrier with the pouch, promoting ease of use and convenience for users seeking simplicity and comfort. In contrast, two-piece bags combine a separate adhesive barrier and pouch, allowing for easier changing of the pouch without removing the skin barrier. This flexibility caters to varying preferences, providing options for patients based on their lifestyle, skin sensitivity, and overall comfort requirements.</p></p>
