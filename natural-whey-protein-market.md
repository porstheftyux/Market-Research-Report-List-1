<p><p>The Natural Whey Protein market comprises mainly two types: Concentrate and Isolate forms. Whey Protein Concentrate typically contains 70-80% protein, retaining beneficial nutrients like fats and carbohydrates, making it more affordable and flavorful. Whey Protein Isolate, on the other hand, undergoes further processing to achieve 90% or higher protein content, with minimal fats and sugars. This form appeals to those seeking higher protein intake with fewer calories, making both types suitable for various dietary needs and fitness goals.</p></p>
