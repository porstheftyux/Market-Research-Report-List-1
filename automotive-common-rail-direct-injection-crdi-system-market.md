<p><strong>The Automotive Common Rail Direct Injection (CRDI) System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SDI</li><li>TDI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Common Rail Direct Injection (CRDI) system market includes two primary types: Saturated Direct Injection (SDI) and Turbo Direct Injection (TDI). SDI systems utilize a common rail to deliver diesel fuel directly into the combustion chamber at low pressure, enhancing efficiency and reducing emissions. TDI systems, on the other hand, combine turbocharging with direct injection for improved performance and power. Both technologies aim to optimize fuel efficiency, reduce emissions, and enhance engine output in modern diesel vehicles.</p></p>
