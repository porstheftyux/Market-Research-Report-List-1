<p><ul><li>Fixed Bracket</li><li>Tracking Bracket</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solar panel mounting system market encompasses two primary types: fixed brackets and tracking brackets. Fixed brackets securely hold solar panels in a stationary position, optimizing efficiency at a specific angle, usually benefiting from lower installation costs and minimal maintenance. In contrast, tracking brackets adjust the panels’ orientation throughout the day, following the sun’s path to maximize solar energy capture, resulting in higher efficiency but increased complexity and costs. Each type serves distinct energy needs and installation scenarios.</p></p>
