<p><p>Cable temperature sensors are specialized devices designed to measure temperature along a length of cable, commonly used in industrial and commercial applications. These sensors are typically comprised of thermocouples or resistance temperature detectors (RTDs) embedded within the cable, allowing continuous temperature monitoring in various environments. Their ability to provide accurate readings over extended distances makes them suitable for applications in power generation, oil and gas, HVAC systems, and manufacturing processes.</p><p>The Cable Temperature Sensors Market is projected to grow at a CAGR of 11.00% during the forecast period.
