<p><p>The automobile exhaust hood market features two primary types: single shell and double shell hoods. Single shell hoods consist of a single layer of material, providing basic protection and functionality for handling exhaust fumes. In contrast, double shell hoods have an additional layer, enhancing thermal insulation and durability while minimizing heat transfer to surrounding areas. This makes double shell hoods more efficient for high-performance environments. Both types are essential for ensuring safety and efficiency in automotive exhaust management.</p></p>
