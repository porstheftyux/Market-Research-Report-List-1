<p><p>The 3D Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) system market is categorized into inline and offline AOI types. Inline AOI integrates seamlessly into manufacturing lines, allowing real-time inspection of printed circuit boards (PCBs) during production, enhancing efficiency and reducing waste. In contrast, offline AOI involves inspecting PCBs separately from the manufacturing process, typically allowing for more detailed analysis and adjustments. Both systems are crucial for ensuring quality control and reliability in electronic manufacturing, each offering unique advantages depending on production needs.</p></p>
